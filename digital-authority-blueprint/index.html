Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to implement? ⬇
The 90-day blueprint is designed so you can start seeing results in 30-60 days. Most founders complete Phase 1 (foundation) in 3-4 weeks. The timeline depends on how much you're implementing yourself vs. hiring help.
What tools do I need? ⬇
The blueprint includes free and paid tool options. You can start with free tools (Mailchimp, Canva, etc.). As you scale, there are paid upgrades recommended. Total startup cost: $0-$500/month depending on your choices.
Is this for agencies or founders? ⬇
Both. The framework works for founders building personal brands, agency owners positioning their business, and executives establishing authority. Examples are tailored to different business types.
Can I customize this for my industry? ⬇
Absolutely. The blueprint is a framework, not a template. Every strategy is customizable to your industry, audience, and business model. That's the whole point—you learn the system and apply it your way.
Do you offer support? ⬇
The blueprint includes resources and templates. You can also book a strategy call if you want personalized guidance on implementation. Most founders successfully implement it on their own.
