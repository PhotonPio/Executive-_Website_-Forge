## INTEGRATION INSTRUCTIONS

### Step 1: Email Service Provider Setup

**Recommended:** Mailchimp, HubSpot, or ConvertKit

1. Create account
2. Add your email address
3. Create 4 email sequences (above)
4. Add form on website to capture emails
5. Connect forms to appropriate sequences

### Step 2: Tagging & Segmentation

Create these tags in your email service:
- `lead-magnet-blueprint` — Downloaded digital authority blueprint
- `website-visitor` — Visited site but didn't convert
- `strategy-call-booked` — Scheduled a call
- `strategy-call-completed` — Completed a call
- `engaged-reader` — Opens 50%+ of emails
- `dormant` — Hasn't opened email in 4 weeks

### Step 3: Automation Setup

**Trigger 1:** Lead Magnet Signup
→ Send Email 1 (Welcome)
→ 3 days later: Send Email 2
→ 5 days later: Send Email 3
→ 7 days later: Send Email 4
→ 7 days later: Send Email 5

**Trigger 2:** Website Form Submission
→ Send Email 1 (12-hour follow-up)
→ 5 days later: Send Email 2
→ 5 days later: Send Email 3
→ 7 days later: Send Email 4
→ Tag as `engaged`

**Trigger 3:** Strategy Call Completed
→ Send Email 1 (24-hour follow-up with roadmap)
→ 3 days later: Send Email 2 (if no reply)
→ 4 days later: Send Email 3 (if no reply)
→ Segment based on response

**Trigger 4:** Weekly
→ Send The Forge Brief every Thursday 9 AM

### Step 4: Metrics to Track

**Daily:**
- New subscribers
- Email opens
- Click-through rate
- Unsubscribe rate

**Weekly:**
- Total subscribers
- Engagement rate (opens + clicks)
- Top-performing subject lines
- Strategy call bookings from email

**Monthly:**
- Conversion rate (lead magnet → strategy call)
- Revenue from email sequences
- List growth rate
- Engagement trend

**Expected Performance:**

```
Lead Magnet Sequence:
- 80%+ open rate on Email 1
- 35-40% conversion to strategy call (Email 5)
- Average: 3-5 strategy calls per 100 signups

Website Visitor Sequence:
- 30-40% open rate
- 10-15% conversion to strategy call
- Average: 1-2 strategy calls per 10 visits

Newsletter:
- 40-50% open rate
- 5-8% click-through rate
- 2-3% conversion to strategy call per month
```

### Step 5: Customization

Edit these emails to match:
- Your voice and personality
- Your specific services and pricing
- Your case studies and testimonials
- Your calendar/booking system
- Your actual name, company, links

**Pro Tips:**

1. **Use personal language** — Write like you're texting a friend, not a corporate email
2. **Include your story** — Why you started the company, what you've learned
3. **Ask questions** — "What's your biggest challenge?" — encourage replies
4. **Make it about them** — Not about your services, about their success
5. **Include specific examples** — Real numbers, real client situations
6. **Keep it short** — 150-250 words per email (except newsletters)
7. **Use clear CTAs** — One main call-to-action per email
8. **Test subject lines** — A/B test to see what works
